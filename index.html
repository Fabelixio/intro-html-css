How to make Pizza!

A simple pizza recipe to nourish your soul!

Pizza is a world famous dish, loved by anyone with common sense. It's easy to make and requires relatively few ingredients! This recipe should take around 30 minutes to prepare and 15 minutes to cook!

Pizza ingredients:

  • 350g strong bread flour
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 2 tsp rapeseed oil
  • half a tsp sea salt
  • ground black pepper
  • 120g passata
  • 1 tbsp tomato puree
  • half a tbsp dried mixed herbs
  • 1 mozzarella ball, torn to pieces

Method:

  1. preheat your oven to 200C/180C Fan/Gas 6
  2. Sift your flower and baking powder into a bowl, add the salt and a pinch of pepper, before stirring in the oil. Stir the warm water into the mix and mix well to create a soft dough
  3. Use the remaining flour to dust a flat surface, then knead the dough in the flour until its smooth and springy
  4. Roll the dough inro a roughly 30cm/12in disk, place into the oven on a baking tray (be careful not to let it stick!) and bake for 3 minutes.
  5. Mix the passata, herbs, sea salt, black pepper and tomato puree to create the sauce.
  6. Remove the dough from the oven and cover its surface in the sauce, leaving a small gap for the crust. Now add your preferred toppings (Pepperoni is my go to!) and spread them evenly.
  7. Bake for 15 minutes. cut into wedges and enjoy!
